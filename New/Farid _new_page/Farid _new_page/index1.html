About Me

I am a fourth year PhD student working under supervision of Professor Venayagamoorthy at Civil and Environmental Engineering Department, Colorado State University. My research focuses on understanding turbulence in wall-bounded flows. I employ both theoretical reasoning and numerical simulations to explore new aspects of wall-bounded turbulence, especially turbulent mixing in the presence of density stratification.
